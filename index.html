Hey, I'm Randeep!

Being born in a city loaded with technology, I developed my keen interest in computer systems at an early age. Once I realized how embedded the computer systems are into our lives, I couldn’t push myself away from it. Due to this affection of mine, I completed my graduation in Computer Science.

During my college time, I was always surprised to see how mobile applications can make our lives easy. Day to day tasks and things to remember are a thing of the past. We can strongly rely on mobile applications for most of the stuff. Then I got to work in a company as an Android Developer, which spearheaded my passion opened the gates of opportunities for me.

My belief is not only should an application be functional but also pleasing for the eyes to see, hence the beauty of an application is as important as the functionality it provides. I believe one-day mobile applications will change the way we live our lives and I’d love to be part of the transition.
